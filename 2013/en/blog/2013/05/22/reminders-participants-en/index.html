Reminders for Participants

2013.05.22 by registration

Dear Participants,

It's a gentle reminder for PyCon Taiwan 2013. It will be held on the 25th and 26th May (Sat. & Sun.) in the International Conference Hall, Humanities & Social Science Building, Academia Sinica, Taipei, Taiwan.

Please see /2013/venue/ for the map.

Check-in

Check-in will be opened at 8:30am.

When you check in, please make sure you have your registration serial number and supplemental papers (e.g., student ID for undergraduate or high school). It helps people at the check-in counter.

Reception & Show & Bazaar

We're pleased to announce that in addition to all the other great things going on - we've added an reception on Saturday May 25th in the evening.

Everyone is invited to attend!

We will have great meal, wonderful show, and socializing.

Oh. Did I mention there will be a "Bazaar of folks"? You can not only be a booth visitor, but also host your own booth!

Performer, booth host are welcomed, please send message to registration+reception@pycon.tw

Internet & Projector

There will be wireless Internet. But if you need more stable connection, please bring a cord.

If you want to give a lightning talk, you are informed that the best resolution of the projectors is 1024x768 VGA. Don't forget an adapter if you need it.

Thank you very much for your support to PyCon Taiwan 2013. We look forward to seeing you.

PyCon Taiwan Operation Committee 2013.
